About Us

We’re not here to explain ourselves. U.N.C.K. Inc wasn’t built for clarity — it emerged, like static between stations, or a beat you only hear in dreams. Some say it’s a brand. Others say it’s a frequency. We’re not correcting anyone. All we know is that it found us, not the other way around.

There’s no manifesto, no timeline, and no pitch deck. Just scattered signals, half-forgotten conversations, and a few too many coincidences. If you’re reading this, chances are you’ve already felt it — the low hum of something you can’t name, but recognize instantly. We don’t do launches. We don't do drops. Things appear when they need to. Or they don’t.
